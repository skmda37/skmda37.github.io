A Little About Me

Hey there, I'm Stefan, and this is my personal website. Currently, I'm pursuing a Computer Science PhD at Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München, where I'm researching interpretable Deep Learning. My goal is to develop AI models that are transparent and interpretable. Since black box models make up the vast majority of deployed deep learning based models, I spent the first two yours of my PhD working on post-hoc explanations of image classifiers. Such methods can help debug models as well as identify potential biases. However, more recently, I shifted to building inherently interpretable models, since I believe black box explanations are fundamentally limited and potentially problematic for safety-critical applications. More broadly my research interests lie in Trustworthy Machine Learning (interpretability, fairness, privacy) and Computer Vision (recognition, 3D reconstruction). I am very open, so please reach out and say hi if you are interested in my research or working together!
