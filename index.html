My goal is to develop AI models that can explain their decisions at a level comparable to humans (the ones that give really good explanations). While there are practical applications that require explainable decisions, I also believe that Artificial Intelligence should eventually be able to provide explanations as well as the best human explainers, as some people are simply curious and enjoy asking "why." I think explainable AI (XAI) and interpretability research has many challenges and problems because good explanations are based on semantics and semantics are hard. In my current research, I am trying to bridge the gap to human-level explanations by using the recent advances in generative modeling (e.g. LLMs or Image Synthesis models) to build AI models that base their decisions on interpretable semantics.
