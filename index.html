A Little About Me

Hi, I'm Stefan, welcome to my personal website! Currently, I'm pursuing a Computer Science Ph.D. at Ludwig-Maximilians-University Munich. My research focuses on Interpretable Machine Learning. A common theme in my PhD research is to use ideas and tools from sparse representations, such as wavelets and dictionary learning, for interpretable machine learning. Early on in my PhD I worked on visualization methods for explainable image classification. More recently, I am working on deep learning based models that are inherenty interpretable by design. Next to my interpretability reserach, I also work on privacy-preserving machine learning.
